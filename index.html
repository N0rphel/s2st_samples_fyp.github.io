Dzongkha & English Speech to Speech Translation

Tshering Norphel, Dorji Phuntsho, Tashi Wangchuk, Sumit Adhikari, Karma Wangchuk

Speech to Speech translation model translates speech from one language to speech in another language. There are two methods which are Cascaded model that uses Automatic Speech Recognition model to generate transcript from audio, Machine Translation Model to translate text in one language to another and then the translated text to speech using a Text-to-Speech model. The other approach is training a end-to-end direct model which is directly capable of translating an input audio to translated audio. Although, our BLUE score is not as great as the subject itself, the samples below demonstrates the output of our research.

Direct S2UT+Vocoder model

S2UT model is trained to predict the discrete units of the target speech where the translated/target audio is synthesized by a HiFi-GAN vocoder

We compare end-to-end model trained with source and target audios with the corresponding reference text.
